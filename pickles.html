Pickles

Ingredients

  • Cucumbers
  • Salt
  • Sugar
  • Vinegar
  • Soju
  • (optional) red pepper seeds

Put cucumbers in an airtight container and sprinkle salt and sugar on them evenly.

Sprinkle vinegar and soju and you’re done! It’s that easy!

You can add red pepper seeds

Seal in airtight container, turn it over once for 10 days, and after 10 days, take the cucumbers and store them in the refrigerator.
