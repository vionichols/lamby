Chicken & Dumplings

from Lisa Nichols

Ingredients

  • for dumplings:
  • 2 cups flour
  • ½ tsp baking soda
  • ½ tsp salt
  • 3 tbsp butter
  • ⅔ cup milk (and more if needed)
  • for chicken & broth:
  • 6 cups chicken broth
  • 1 onion, cut in half
  • 2-3 carrots
  • 2-3 celery stalks
  • Bone-in, skin-on chicken

Dumplings:

*Make 1st & allow to dry out a bit*

Mix flour, baking soda, salt

Cut in butter, add milk

Mix

On parchment paper, roll out dough ¼ inch thick

With pizza cutter, cut 1”x3” strips

Chicken & broth:

Mix chicken broth, onion, carrots, celery, and chicken and cook in a large pot on the stove for an hour on medium heat

When chicken is cooked through, remove chicken, onion, carrots, and celery

Pick chicken off bone and put it aside

Discard (onion, carrots, celery) bones, skin

Drop dumplings in simmering broth, a little at a time & stir (so they don’t stick together)

Cook 7-10 minutes

Add shredded chicken in

Cook on low until thickened, ~20 minutes

Add salt & pepper
