Garlic salmon

Serves 2

Ingredients

  • 1 salmon filet
  • 1 bulb of garlic
  • 1 lemon
  • 1 tsp- 1 tbsp dijon mustard
  • Salt
  • Butter
  • Olive oil
  • Optional: fresh thyme, dill

Mince whole bulb of garlic

Squeeze/juice one lemon (optional: zest!)

Combine garlic, lemon juice, mustard, and some olive oil (optional: thyme, dill) in a freezer bag.

Dry and salt salmon

Add salmon to the bag and combine. Let sit for 10 minutes.

Put a slice of butter and a bit of olive oil in a pan on medium-low heat.

When butter is melted, cook salmon skin side first and put sauce on top. Cook for 4 minutes.

Flip salmon. Cook for 4 minutes/until the internal temperature is 125 degrees.

Deglaze pan and top with sauce.
