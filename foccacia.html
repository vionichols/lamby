Foccacia

Ingredients

  • 3 ¾ cups AP flour
  • ⅜ tsp yeast
  • slightly less than 2 cups room temperature (70-90 degrees) water
  • ¾ tsp sugar
  • ¾ tsp salt
  • extra virgin olive oil
  • fresh rosemary
  • garlic powder
  • thyme
  • sea salt

Add flour to mixer, use dough hook. Gradually add water.

Mix until dough comes together, ~3 minutes.

Increase mixer speed to #4, beat it until smooth, shiny ball, ~20 minutes

Add sugar and salt and beat until well incorporated, ~3 minutes.

Let dough rise. Put a dash of EVOO in bowl to lightly grease dough, turn, and cover. Allow dough to double, ~4 hours.

Shape dough and let it rise.

Drizzle EVOO onto sheet pan (with parchment paper)

Pour dough onto greased sheet pan.

Coat your hand with oil and spread dough as thin as possible without tearing it.

Let it relax for 10 minutes, then spread it to fill the entire sheet, trying to maintain the bubbles in the dough.

Cover the pan with a plastic wrap and allow it to rise until 1 ½ times its original volume, ~1 hour.

Preheat oven to 475 degrees one hour before baking

Put baking stone on lowest level of oven.

Sprinkle on toppings.

Drizzle more EVOO on rosemary, garlic, thyme, sea salt.

Bake 12-13 minutes or until top is golden.
