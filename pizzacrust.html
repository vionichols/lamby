Pizza crust

Ingredients

  • 1 cup water
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 2 ¼ tsp active dry yeast
  • 3 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 2 ½ cups AP flour
  • ¼ tsp garlic powder
  • ¼ tsp onion powder
  • ¼ tsp oregano

Bring water to 105-115 degrees.

Stir water, sugar, and yeast until dissolved.

Add olive oil and salt.

Let dough rest 10 minutes.

Bake on pizza stone for ~7 minutes.
