Chicken pot pie

Ingredients

  • 4 chicken breasts, bone-in, skin-on
  • (optional) 1 large baking potato
  • 2 carrots
  • ½ cup flour
  • 1 tsp thyme
  • ½ tsp onion powder
  • 1 stick butter
  • 2 cups chicken broth
  • ½ cup milk
  • ¼ tsp pepper
  • 1 tsp marjoram
  • Pillsbury pie crust

Boil chicken (reserve broth).

Cool and tear into pieces.

Peel and chop potato and carrots.

Cook until fork-tender.

Melt butter on low heat. Take your time.

Blend in flour, pepper, thyme, marjoram, onion powder, and a little salt.

Stir until smooth and bubbly.

Remove from heat.

Stir in chicken broth and milk.

Return to heat.

Heat to boiling, stirring constantly. Boil and stir for 1 minute.

Dip in a piece of chicken and taste it. Adjust seasoning as needed.

Stir in chicken and vegetables.

Preheat oven to 425 degrees.

Put together pie crust.

Pinch edges.

Add steam hole and slits.

Cook uncovered 30-35 minutes until crust is golden-brown and delicious.
