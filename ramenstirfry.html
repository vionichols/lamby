Ramen stir-fry

Ingredients

  • 1 pack of ramen noodles
  • 1 egg
  • (optional) 1 green onion
  • 3 tsp soy sauce
  • 1 tsp hoisin sauce
  • 1 tsp honey
  • 1 tsp fish sauce
  • Sesame oil
  • 1 tsp garlic, powder or minced
  • 1 tsp crushed red pepper
  • Sesame seeds
  • (optional) veggies of your choice :)

Put on a pot of water to boil for ramen.

Put a dash of oil in a skillet and turn the stove on medium-low heat.

Crack and stir an egg and toss it into the skillet, scrambling it.

Add 1 tsp soy sauce and a dash of sesame oil.

When water comes to a boil, cook the ramen noodles.

Sauce: mix soy sauce, hoisin sauce, honey, fish sauce, a dash of sesame oil, garlic, and crushed red peppers.

When noodles are done, combine with eggs and sauce in a pan, along with vegetables of your choice.

Serve with sesame seeds and green onions.
