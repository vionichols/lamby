Waffle batter

Ingredients

  • 1 ¾ cup flour
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tbsp baking powder
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 ¾ cups milk
  • ½ oil or melted butter
  • 1 tsp vanilla

Combine ingredients in order listed

Stir until just moistened

Refrigerate until using
