Stir-fry mushrooms

Ingredients

  • (baby) king oyster mushrooms
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp brown sugar

Chop mushrooms in half or quarters

Sautee mushrooms in pan

Push to side

Add 1 tbsp soy sauce and BURN it

Add 1 tbsp brown sugar

Mix

Mix mushroom back in
